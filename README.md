# AULA S98 Pro Wireless Companion

Take total control of your typing experience on macOS. The AULA S98 Pro Wireless Companion is a lightweight, open-source utility built to configure and manage your AULA S98 Pro mechanical keyboard natively on your Mac.

### Key Features:
* **Smart Screen Management:** Synchronize your Mac's system clock, date, and battery monitoring with the keyboard's built-in TFT LCD screen seamlessly over wireless connections.
* **On-Board Media Control:** Ensure the multi-function control knob interacts flawlessly with macOS volume and system settings.
* **Custom GIF & Image Uploads:** Import, process, and upload custom animations directly to your keyboard's display screen right from your Mac.
* **Local Profiles:** Remap keys and handle macro layouts locally without changing core hardware firmware properties.
* **Privacy-First Design:** Operates 100% locally on your machine. Does not require an internet connection, account registration, or data tracking.


# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs your use of our macOS software application and any associated keyboard driver or extension (collectively, the **"Application"**). The Application functions entirely locally on your device to enable keyboard inputs, layouts, or modifications.

### 1. No Data Collection (Zero-Data Policy)
We believe in complete privacy. The Application **does not collect, store, transmit, or monitor** any user data.

* **Keystroke Privacy:** The Application processes your keystrokes and inputs in real time strictly to execute core input features. It never records, caches, logs, or saves your keystrokes.
* **Personal & Device Data:** We do not collect names, email addresses, device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data.
* **No Accounts Required:** You can use the Application without registering an account, logging in, or providing any personal details.

### 2. Input Monitoring Permissions
To function correctly on macOS, the Application requires system permissions under **System Settings > Privacy & Security > Input Monitoring**.

* This macOS permission is technically necessary for the driver to recognize hardware key events.
* While macOS warns that apps with this permission can monitor your input, **our Application never saves, logs, or transmits this data**. All key translation and processing happen entirely in memory on your local machine.

### 3. Third-Party Access & Networking
Because the Application collects no data, there is no data to share.

* We do not include any third-party advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or telemetry tools.
* The Application does not require internet access to function and does not transmit data over the network.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
The Application does not collect or transmit any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is fully compliant with child privacy laws worldwide.
